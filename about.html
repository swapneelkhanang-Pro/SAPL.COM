Our Journey

Source Analytics Private Limited was founded in 2016 by Late Sri Mr. Sunil Sangwekar and Mrs. Manasi Khanang, with a vision to create a dependable industrial solutions company that integrates engineering capability with efficient supply management.

Today, the company is led by its Board of Directors: Mrs. Manasi Khanang and Mrs. Kalpana Kulkarni. Under their leadership, SAPL has strengthened its operational capabilities and expanded across multiple industrial verticals.
